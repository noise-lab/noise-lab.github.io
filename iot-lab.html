Internet of Things (IoT) Lab

The IoT Lab serves as a resource for UChicago students and faculty to experiment with cutting-edge devices and datasets for research and applications.

The lab studies security, privacy, and network performance by replicating home broadband networks in a laboratory setting. It hosts several IoT devices powered by CableLabs with an isolated, dedicated Comcast internet connection.

Research Questions

  • "Am I getting what I paid for from my internet subscription?"
  • "What happens when one of your home connected devices is compromised?"
  • "What exactly happens when you play your favorite Netflix video?"
  • "How can we watch the watchers?"
